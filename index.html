Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our painting services

How long does a typical painting project take?

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. A standard interior room typically takes 1-2 days, while whole-house exteriors may take 3-5 days. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and always strive to complete projects on schedule.

What type of paint do you use?

We exclusively use premium paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. These high-quality paints provide superior coverage, durability, and color retention, ensuring your investment lasts for years to come.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide completely free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. Our detailed quotes include materials, labor, timeline, and any additional services needed. There are never any hidden fees or surprise costs.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Celestial Painting Inc. is fully licensed, bonded, and insured with comprehensive liability coverage. This protects both your property and our team, giving you complete peace of mind throughout your project.

What's included in your warranty?

We offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all workmanship and a satisfaction guarantee. If any issues arise due to our workmanship, we'll return to fix them at no charge. We stand behind our work 100%.
